About me

I’m Danish Sayyed, a Senior Data Analyst based in Mumbai who specializes in turning "messy" data into clear, actionable stories. With over 6 years of experience in high-stakes environments, I’ve learned that data isn't just about numbers—it’s about making sure the people in charge don't have to guess what to do next.

My career has been a bit of a high-wire act. At Hawk-Eye Innovations, I managed data for over 220 televised matches. When you're dealing with live broadcasts and DRS operations, an "oops" moment isn't just a bug—it’s a national conversation. Maintaining a 98% accuracy rate under that kind of latency has given me a very healthy respect for precision (and perhaps a few grey hairs).

Before I was analyzing ball-tracking data, I was a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Mumbai. These days, I’ve swapped wrenches for SQL, Python, and Tableau. Whether it’s building predictive models or automating Tableau dashboards that save management 30% of their manual effort, I thrive on solving complex problems that actually move the needle.

When I’m not staring at a dashboard:

  • Deep-diving into cricket analytics (yes, I do this for fun too)
  • Experimenting with AI image generation to create cinematic portraits
  • Explaining that "data-driven" isn’t just a buzzword, it’s a mindset
